In the action-packed original story of Marvel's Spider-Man Remastered, Peter Parker's and Spider-Man's worlds collide. Play as an experienced Peter Parker, fighting against major crimes and iconic villains in Marvel's New York City.

The year is 1989.

"Be even more magnificent When Symbolic Marvel villains threaten the city of New York, Peter Parker's and Spider-Man's worlds collide. For Peter to save his city and loved ones, he must stand on his own two feet and become even more magnificent."

In Marvel's Spider-Man Remastered, after eight years behind the mask, Peter Parker has become a seasoned crime-fighter. Experience the full power of a more experienced Spider-Man with improvised combat, dynamic and acrobatic movements, seamless city traversal, and engaging environmental interactions. Feel like Spider-Man as you swing through the city, taking down iconic villains and fighting crime with all of your abilities and skills.

Worlds Collide Peter Parker's and Spider-Man's worlds collide in an action-packed original story. In this new Spider-Man universe, iconic characters from Peter and Spider-Man's lives are reimagined, taking on unique roles while still being familiar faces.

Marvel's New York City becomes your playground in Marvel's Spider-Man. Swing through vibrant neighborhoods and witness breathtaking views of iconic locations in Manhattan as you web-sling your way around. In this impressive action game, use the environment to take down villains in epic fashion and make the city safe from evil.
